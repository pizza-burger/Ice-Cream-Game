Instructions

Click "New Game" at the top of the page to begin a new game. Try to match the ice cream flavours shown on the top right by clicking on the ice cream dispensers. Each time you click a dispenser, you add a scoop of that flavour to your cone (shown on the bottom). When you are done, click on the "Done!" button and continue matching the new scoops on the top left. Click on "Reset Scoop" if you think you made a mistake. Try to match as many ice cream scoops as you can within 60 seconds!
